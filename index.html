Our mission

“We understand that there is a big barrier for women to enter the tech world, which consists of sex biases, lack of exposure to tech and difficult access to resources.


NoDress.Code was created to dimish this barrier through providing women with mentorship support and guidance, in order for them to become role-models for other girls.”

Yuliia, Founder of NoDress.Code

Who can participate

Driven to change yourself ?

You should have a clear idea in mind and be driven to change yourself, prior to changing the world.


If that is you, apply, and let’s chat ;)

It just takes 5 minutes to apply!

Apply for the next cohort in January 2025.
